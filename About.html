About ACMxPCCOER

PCCOER ACM Student Chapter has been founded for the technical development for students in the field of computing.The Chapter provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.It facilitates community and individual growth in the computing profession.The Chapter organizes a number of events,seminars,talks,and workshop for its members. It aims to promote Computer Science Culture.
